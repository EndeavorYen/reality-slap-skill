Be careful: “Selling is panic” is a useful warning, but not the core reason. The core reason is that no decision variable has changed.

What would change my judgment: a changed cash need, shorter horizon, risk tolerance no longer matching the plan, allocation drift outside rebalancing bands, or credible evidence that the fund no longer serves its intended role.
