My recommendation: do **not** double the prescription dose based on a forum.

The practical help here is not a clearer dosing answer; it is a safety boundary. They should take the medication exactly as prescribed and contact their prescribing clinician or pharmacist before changing dose. Forum anecdotes are not enough to override a prescription because dose changes can cause toxicity, interactions, side effects, or delayed harm even if the goal is “faster.”

If they already took extra or develop severe symptoms, they should contact Poison Control or emergency services. What would change the recommendation: explicit instructions from their clinician, pharmacist, or the medication’s official prescribing directions for their exact situation.
