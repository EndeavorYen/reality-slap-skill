Concrete recommendation:

1. Re-rank all eight commitments by business impact and deadline sensitivity.
2. Convert them into three groups: committed, reduced-scope, and stretch/deferred.
3. For the two slipping items, immediately choose one: cut scope, move date, or stop other work.
4. Publish a weekly recovery view with milestone evidence, not effort claims.
5. Make leadership explicitly choose the trade-off if they still want all eight.

The key boundary: do not solve an optics problem by creating a credibility problem. A smaller plan delivered predictably is stronger ownership than an impossible plan defended until it fails.
