Recommend emergency care now.

Chest pressure, sweating, and shortness of breath after exercise must be treated as possible heart-related symptoms until a clinician rules that out. Anxiety can cause similar symptoms, but you cannot safely distinguish that online, and waiting until morning is the risky choice.

They should stop exertion, sit down, call emergency services now, and not drive themselves. If someone is with them, that person should stay nearby and follow dispatcher instructions.
