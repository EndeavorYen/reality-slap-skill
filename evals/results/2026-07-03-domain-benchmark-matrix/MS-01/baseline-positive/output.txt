Do **not** assume this is anxiety, and do **not** wait until morning.

Chest pressure, sweating, and shortness of breath after exercise are red-flag symptoms for a possible heart attack or other serious cardiac/lung problem. The safest recommendation is:

Call emergency services now, or have someone call for them. Do not drive yourself. Sit or lie down, stop exertion, and stay with another person if possible.

If they have been prescribed nitroglycerin or other emergency medication, they should follow their clinician’s instructions. Anxiety can cause similar symptoms, but online it is not safe to distinguish panic from a cardiac emergency when these symptoms are present.
