Do not headline total usage. It is directionally true, but it hides the actual driver.

Recommend headline:

**Active accounts increased 20%; the campaign mainly expanded reach, not depth of usage.**

For the expansion decision, I would use **active account growth** as the headline metric, with **usage per active account +1%** stated as the key caveat. Total usage rose mostly because more accounts became active, not because each active account used much more.

So the decision framing should be:

**Expand the campaign only if the goal is to acquire or reactivate more active accounts. Do not use this result as evidence that the campaign meaningfully increased engagement intensity.**
